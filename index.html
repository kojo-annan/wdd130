Ghana

Ghana, located in West Africa, is renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning natural landscapes. Known as the "Gold Coast" during colonial times, Ghana boasts beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, and bustling urban centers like Accra, its capital city. With a diverse population of over 30 million people, Ghana is celebrated for its hospitality, colorful festivals, and traditional crafts such as kente weaving and bead-making. As a stable democracy with a growing economy, Ghana plays a key role in the region, balancing modern advancements with its deep-rooted heritage.
